Book Recommendations

"On The Road" By Jack Kerouac

"On The Road" is a novel by Jack Kerouac that focuses on the travels and interactions of a man named Sal Paradise and friends journeying back and forth across the United States. Sal a writer from New York encounters many people all in the throws of their own stories on his adventure. Written in a biographical style the author uses their own experiences to carve out this story. Throughout Kerouac encapsulates the beats generation and all the cultural phenomenon that formed it throughout this book. If you enjoy odd fast-paced stories based in reality with fictionalized elements and spectacular imagery then I would definitely recommend it.

"The Mist" By Stephen King

"The Mist" is a novella from the great mind of Stephen King that reflects the fear of the unknown. It follows David Drayton a painter living in Maine with his wife and son when a strange mist approaches from the lake by their house. Within it lurks cosmic horrors humans may not even be able to process. If your a fan of monster horror as well as analysis of how high fear environments impacts social dynamics and mental health then this may be the book for you.
